About

Hi, my name is Maria and this is my blog about plants!

When I moved on my own in 2016, I always dreamed of having lots of plants in my home. I had no previous experience with it, so it started as a small hobby. I will honestly admit that in the early years I killed a lot of plants, and I still kill some to this day. But everything is a learning curve, and I have learned a lot by reading up. Now I have a home with nice plants, and I have had several of them for several years.

In this blog I will show tips and tricks from my own experience and from various sources
